  • Commissions

    Commissioning a portrait is a very individual process and each piece is tailored to your personal requirements.

    I believe it is essential to gain an in-depth understanding of each subject and the relationship between subject and owner which helps me to truly capture personality and spirit.

    When commissioning a portrait, please bear in mind that the best quality, highest resolution photographs are essential to allowing me to achieve the closest likeness. I am always happy to discuss photography options and choices with you during the initial stages of booking your commission.

    Upon agreeing on a portrait size, reference photographs, price and timescale, a deposit of 25% is required to secure your booking.
